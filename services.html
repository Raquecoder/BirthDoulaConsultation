Services

I offer emotional and physical support during pregnancy, birth, and postpartum. My goal is to ensure mothers and families in Seattle experience a fulfilling prenatal and birth journey — and feel secure and loved along the way.

Birth Planning

I'm committed to helping you navigate the exceptionally personal and magical journey into parenthood! As you search for a doula, you need someone with experience, expertise, and compassion. No matter your background, I am here to support you.

Prenatal Support

From week to week, or month to month, you might have questions along the way of your pregnancy journey. Let me support you.

Pregnancy Doula Services

During pregnancy, especially if it’s your first child. You may feel overwhelmed by the amount of resources out there and finding a source for accurate answers. Come no further. We are here to support you. This includes the proper education during the pregnancy process, all the way through postpartum life.
